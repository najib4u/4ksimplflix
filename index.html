Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our IPTV service

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers television content over the internet instead of through traditional terrestrial, satellite, or cable formats. With 4KSimplFlix, you can stream live TV channels and on-demand content directly to your device using our dedicated apps or compatible media players. All you need is a stable internet connection and a compatible device.

We recommend the following minimum internet speeds for optimal streaming:

  • SD Quality: 5 Mbps
  • HD Quality: 10 Mbps
  • Full HD 1080p: 15 Mbps
  • 4K Ultra HD: 25 Mbps

For multiple simultaneous streams, multiply these requirements by the number of devices you'll be using at the same time.

Setup is quick and easy:

  1. Download our app from the appropriate app store or our website
  2. Install the app on your device
  3. Launch the app and enter your login credentials
  4. Start enjoying your content immediately

We provide detailed setup guides for all supported devices in our Knowledge Base.
